# Synthesia

## What it is
Synthesia is an AI video generation platform that creates professional-looking videos featuring realistic AI avatars from simple text scripts.

## What problem it solves
Eliminates the need for cameras, microphones, actors, or studio space when producing talking-head videos for training, communication, or marketing.

## Where it fits in the stack
AI & Knowledge — used for automated video presentation and educational content.

## Typical use cases
- Creating corporate training and onboarding videos
- Producing personalized sales outreach videos
- Multilingual video content (avatars can "speak" over 120 languages)
- Educational explainers and news-style updates

## Strengths
- Highly realistic avatar movement and lip-syncing
- Ease of use; no video editing experience required
- Significant cost and time savings compared to traditional video shoots

## Limitations
- Avatars may still lack the nuanced emotional range of human actors
- Content is restricted by the platform's ethical guidelines and moderation
- High-quality custom avatars require an additional setup and fee

## When to use it
- For high-volume, script-based video content where human presence is needed but a full production is impractical
- When you need to quickly localize video content into multiple languages

## When not to use it
- For high-impact cinematic storytelling or content requiring deep emotional nuance
- When you want to maintain a "hand-crafted" or raw human feel for a brand
