# Runway ML

## What it is
Runway (specifically Runway Gen-3 Alpha and its suite of tools) is an AI-powered creative platform focused on video generation, editing, and professional media production.

## What problem it solves
Lowers the barrier to high-quality video production by allowing users to generate video from text or images, and providing advanced AI-assisted editing tools like rotoscoping and inpainting.

## Where it fits in the stack
AI & Knowledge — used for generative media and advanced video processing.

## Typical use cases
- Text-to-video and image-to-video generation
- AI-assisted video editing (e.g., removing objects, background replacement)
- Motion brush and camera control for static images
- Rapid prototyping for filmmakers and creative agencies

## Strengths
- Leading-edge generative video quality
- Professional-grade tools integrated into a web-based interface
- Active research and frequent updates to model capabilities

## Limitations
- High computational cost (requires "credits" or high-tier subscriptions)
- Steep learning curve for advanced features
- Generative artifacts are still common in complex scenes

## When to use it
- When creating cinematic B-roll or experimental video content
- For rapid visual effects tasks that would manually take hours (like rotoscoping)

## When not to use it
- For simple video hosting or basic trimming tasks
- When you have no budget for cloud-based rendering credits
