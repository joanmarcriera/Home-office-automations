# Homebox

## What it is
Homebox is an inventory and organization system built for the home user. It focuses on simplicity and ease of use.

## What problem it solves
It helps you keep track of physical items in your homelab and household, including where they are, what they cost, and their warranty status.

## Where it fits in the pipeline
**Store / Ingest**

## Typical use cases (in this homelab / family automation context)
- **Homelab Asset Tracking**: Recording serial numbers, purchase dates, and specs for servers and networking gear.
- **Home Insurance**: Maintaining a digital record of valuable items for insurance purposes.
- **Manuals Storage**: Attaching PDF manuals to household appliances for easy access.

## Integration points
- **REST API**: For integrating with custom dashboards or automation scripts.
- **CSV Import/Export**: For bulk data management.
