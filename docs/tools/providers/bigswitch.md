# BigSwitch

BigSwitch is a community-driven directory focused on European alternatives to Big Tech. It promotes digital sovereignty by highlighting GDPR-compliant, EU-owned, and often open-source tools.

## When to use it

- When seeking tech providers that operate under European jurisdiction and GDPR.
- When digital sovereignty and local data residency are priority requirements.
- For finding alternatives to dominant US-based SaaS platforms.

## Key Features

- **EU-Centric**: Focuses exclusively on European technology companies and services.
- **GDPR Compliance**: Highlights tools that prioritize European data protection standards.
- **Community-Curated**: A directory built and maintained by contributors focused on digital sovereignty.
- **Open Search**: Provides a searchable index of alternatives across various categories (Cloud, Productivity, Communication, etc.).

## Getting started

Visit [bigswitch.eu](https://bigswitch.eu/) to browse the directory and discover European alternatives for your tech stack.
